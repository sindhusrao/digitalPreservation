The Herur House

 

This 80 year old house lives with an essence of pleasant past, warmly inviting it's family members from abroad, fellow villagers and even a passer by.

Kutgod Mane

 

Standing on the foothills of western ghats,

this 150 year old house is now unoccupied.

The picturesque set up has engulfed this house keeping it away from  its family and visitors.

Putmurthy Mane

 

Hidden behind a buzzing shoping complex in a busy temple town, is a gem. For over a hundred year it has housed a big family now left with an proud old couple.
