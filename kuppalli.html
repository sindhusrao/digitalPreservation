Kavi mane is famous for being the birthplace and childhood home of the renowned Kannada playwright and poet Kuvempu. Kavimane is the ancestral house of Kuvempu. Kavimane means House of the poet in Kannada language. Nestled in the midst of green forests of Malnad, the house presents a scintillating view.
